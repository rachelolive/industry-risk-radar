Methodology

How the risk score is built

0.40 × volume momentum  (30d vs 90d baseline)
0.35 × negativity  (share of negative coverage)
0.25 × event severity  (log of peak magnitude)

Rolled up across eight risk categories, volume-weighted, then normalised to a 0–100 scale.
